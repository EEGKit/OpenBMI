Example 1: Instructions

Example 1. Illustrating how to make an instruction.

Most important commands:
<h1>...</h1> for headings
<p> for starting a new paragraph
<br> for line breaks
<center></center> for centering content
<img src="mypic.png"> for including images
<hr> for horizontal lines

A complete list can be found here: http://docs.wxwidgets.org/trunk/overview_html.html
